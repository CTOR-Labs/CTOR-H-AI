CTOR

Toroidal Strategy · Two Players
Mouse Controls
PUT Click [Put] button, then left-click any empty cell to place your piece. Up to 2×.
MOVE Click [Move] button, then drag your piece to an adjacent empty cell. Up to 2×.
REPLACE Click [Replace] button, click 2 of your pieces to remove, then click empty cell for new piece. 1×.
END TURN Click [End Turn] to finish your turn and let the AI move.
EATING A piece is captured automatically if surrounded by 5+ enemy pieces in its 3×3 neighbourhood.
